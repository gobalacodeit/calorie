Nutrition Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Sometimes thirst is mistaken for hunger.

Eat Whole Foods

Focus on vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, and whole grains for sustained energy.

Meal Timing

Eat regular meals to maintain stable blood sugar and avoid overeating.

Quality Sleep

Poor sleep affects hormones that control hunger. Aim for 7-9 hours nightly.
